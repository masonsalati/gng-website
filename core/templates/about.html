About GnG

GnG was founded three years ago by Mason Salati and Mirco Fiorin. What began as door-to-door visits to help fund our university tuition quickly turned into something much bigger.

Through hard work, determination, and a commitment to delivering top-quality window services, we built GnG into a respected company with many satisfied residential and commercial clients.

After multiple successful summers of professional service, we continue to grow our reputation based on reliability, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction.

Mason Salati

Mason is currently a Computer Science major and the developer behind the GnG website. Combining technical skill with entrepreneurial drive, he plays a key role in building the company’s digital presence while maintaining a strong commitment to hard work and service excellence.

Mirco Fiorin

Mirco is currently an International Business major and serves as the lead sales representative for GnG. His communication skills and client-first approach have been instrumental in expanding the company’s customer base and building lasting relationships.
